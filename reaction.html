Chemical Reactions



  • Direct Combination Reaction

    A + B → AB

    Types :

    • Element 1 + Element 2 → Compound
    • Compound + Element → New Compound
    • Compund 1 + Compound 2 → New Compound

  • Decomposition Reaction

    Some Types of Thermal Decomposition :

    • Decomposition of metal carbonates
    • Decomposition of metal hydroxides
    • Decomposition of metal oxides
    • Decomposition of metal nitrates

    Some other Decomposition reactions include :
    • Electrolytic decomposition
    • Thermal dissociation
    • Photochemical decomposition

  • Simple Displacement Reaction

    Categories :

    • Reaction of active metal with dilute acid
    • Reaction of active metal with water
    • Reaction of a metal with salt of another metal

  • Double Decomposition Reaction

    Categories :

    • Neutralization reaction
    • Precipitation reaction
